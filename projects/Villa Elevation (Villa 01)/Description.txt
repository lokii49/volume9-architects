Contemporary Villa Elevation (Villa 01)

Villa 01 is a contemporary residential design that embodies clarity of form, refined materiality, and a balanced architectural composition. The elevation is defined by strong horizontal lines and clean vertical accents, creating a modern yet timeless visual identity.

The façade features a sophisticated interplay of materials—smooth white surfaces, dark accent frames, and warm wooden elements—that together create depth and contrast. Vertical louvers and textured panels introduce rhythm and softness, while maintaining a minimalist aesthetic.
