An Expression of Space, Light & Refined Living

This residence is conceived as a fluid composition of spaces, where architecture and interiors come together to create a seamless living experience. The design moves beyond aesthetics, focusing on how each space is perceived, experienced, and lived in.
At the heart of the home lies a dramatic double-height volume, where a sculptural staircase and cascading chandelier define the spatial identity.
This vertical connection not only enhances openness but also creates a striking visual focal point.
The living, dining, and kitchen areas unfold as a continuous environment—balanced through clean lines, warm materials, and subtle lighting. Bedrooms offer a contrast to this openness, becoming quiet, intimate retreats with soft textures and calming palettes.

Details are carefully considered—from bespoke wardrobes and vanity spaces to layered lighting that shapes mood and depth throughout the home .
