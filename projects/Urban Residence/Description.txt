Contemporary Urban Residence

An Expression of Light, Texture & Vertical Elegance

This residence is envisioned as a sculpted composition of volumes—where architecture is shaped not just by form, but by the way light interacts with it. The façade rises with a sense of quiet confidence, defined by strong vertical elements and softened by layers of material and greenery.

A monolithic stone-clad feature wall anchors the design, catching light and casting shadows that evolve throughout the day. In contrast, warm wooden soffits and subtle horizontal textures introduce balance and warmth. The glass-enclosed stair volume becomes a moment of transparency—revealing movement within while maintaining a sense of privacy.

As seen in the elevation, lighting is not an addition but an integral part of the architecture. Carefully placed fixtures highlight surfaces, edges, and planters, transforming the home into a dynamic visual experience after dusk.

Balconies extend outward as green pockets, blurring the line between built form and nature, while the terrace becomes a quiet retreat framed by sky and landscape.

This is a home designed with restraint and intention—where every line, material, and light source contributes to a cohesive architectural narrative. A space that feels modern, yet timeless.
