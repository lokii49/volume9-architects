Project Description – Mrs. Vinay Reddy Residence

Mrs. Vinay Reddy Residence is a contemporary home elevation designed to express elegance through clean geometry, balanced proportions, and refined material contrast. The façade presents a modern architectural identity with a strong vertical composition, layered volumes, and carefully articulated surface details that give the residence a distinctive and memorable street presence.
The design combines smooth white planes, textured grey surfaces, warm wooden soffits, and decorative terracotta jaali panels to create depth and visual interest. 
Glass railings and open terrace edges introduce lightness to the overall mass, while landscaped elements soften the hard architecture and connect the residence to its natural surroundings. The result is a home that feels modern, welcoming, and timeless—where function, comfort, and visual identity are seamlessly integrated.
